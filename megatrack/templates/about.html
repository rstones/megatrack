The MegaTrack Atlas is an online neuroimaging tool developed in the Natbrainlab which is part of the Sackler Institute at King's College London.


The tool provides access to white matter dissections of diffusion MRI data from the BRC Atlas dataset. Users can query subjects based on certain demographics (age, gender etc) and view averaged density maps for different white matter tracts. Statistical metrics for the averaged tracts are also generated.


We gratefully acknowledge funding from the NIHR Biomedical Research Centre at South London and Maudsley NHS Foundation Trust as well as the Wellcome Trust.
